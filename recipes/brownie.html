Brownie recipe

Image credit: PicWish, Pixabay.

Description

A quick and easy brownie dessert that can be made in just a few minutes in your own oven.

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 cups chocolate powder
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • 12 tablespoons flour

    • Steps

  • Step 1Melt the butter and set aside
  • Step 2While the butter is melting, combine the 3 eggs and sugar and mix well.
  • Step 3Add the melted butter to the egg and sugar.
  • Step 4Now just mix in the chocolate powder and flour.
  • Step 5Grease a baking pan with butter and chocolate powder.
  • Step 6Bake in the oven at 180Â°C for 30 minutes.
